Review Sentiment Analysis

Understanding customer sentiments for better engagement

Working of the model

In the realm of customer service and feedback management, a crucial process involves the collection of call recording transactions or review texts. These raw data pieces serve as the foundation for understanding customer sentiment. Through the implementation of sophisticated sentiment analysis models, the collected data is meticulously scrutinized and classified based on the emotional tone conveyed within. Once the sentiment of the texts is accurately determined, strategies for effective response formulation can be devised. This pivotal step ensures that replies to the reviews are crafted with precision and empathy, fostering positive customer experiences and bolstering brand reputation.
